How to use this scenario engine

The Premise: Elections are often decided by the people who stayed home or voted for third parties. This tool lets you play campaign manager and see what happens if you mobilize them.

1. The Sliders

Every state has two sliders. The top slider controls the Did Not Vote (White) pool. The bottom slider controls the Third Party (Yellow) pool. Drag the handles left or right to shift those voters to the Blue or Red candidate.

2. The Pro-Tip (Double Clicks!)

Don't want to guess? Double-click the left half of any slider to automatically calculate the exact minimum number of voters needed to flip that state Blue. Double-click the right half to flip it Red.

3. Bulk Actions

Use the control panels on the left to apply broad national strategies. Click "Min Flip to Blue" to let the algorithm mathematically find the easiest path to 270 Electoral Votes using the fewest possible voters.
